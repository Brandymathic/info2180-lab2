My Interest in Computer Science


October 3, 2024, by Brandon Ellington

My drive for Computer Science came about when I had finished High School. I discoverd that I had a strength in understanding and manipulating computer from a tender age. Wether it was through helping my friends or family members with issues that they may have had in regards to using their devices.



In general I tended to have a genuine interest in different parts and functions of computers. When I had discovered that I had this interest is when I did Information Technology as a subject in grade 11. Then this fascination began to grow as I did Computer Science as a subject in CAPE unit 1 and CAPE unit 2.








My Interest in Website Building


October 1, 2024, by Brandon Ellington

As it drew closer to the end of the second semester in my first year of university. I began to realise that I had an interest in website building. It is rather engaging, and fun to me. Not only do I want to pursue Web Development because it is fun, but it can also be profitable to me in the future.

I think that it is important to have some form of education in web development. In this day and age it is very beneficial to have knowledge in computers and programming. It can help you to grow your personal business or be used for your own personal use.
